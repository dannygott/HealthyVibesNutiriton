How We Help Resolve Issues with Leaky Gut

As a certified nutritionist, I know firsthand how frustrating and debilitating leaky gut syndrome can be.

Leaky gut occurs when the lining of the small intestine becomes damaged, allowing harmful substances to leak into the bloodstream and trigger inflammation throughout the body.

Fortunately, there are many ways that I can help you address your leaky gut and restore your health and vitality.

Getting Right to the Cause

I can help you identify which foods and drinks may be contributing to your leaky gut. Certain foods, such as gluten, dairy, and sugar, can be particularly damaging to the intestinal lining and should be avoided. By working with me, we can develop a customized diet plan that eliminates these harmful foods and includes plenty of nutrient-dense whole foods to support gut healing.

Supplementing Your Health

In addition to dietary changes, I can also recommend specific supplements and herbs to support gut healing and reduce inflammation. For example, probiotics, digestive enzymes, and L-glutamine are all beneficial for gut health and can help repair the intestinal lining. I can also recommend herbs such as slippery elm and marshmallow root, which have soothing and protective properties for the digestive tract.

Small Lifestyle Changes

I can provide you with guidance on lifestyle changes that can support gut healing. Stress, poor sleep, and lack of exercise can all contribute to leaky gut and inflammation, so I can help you develop strategies to manage these factors and improve your overall health.

How to Get Started

I am here to help. As a certified nutritionist, I can provide you with personalized guidance on which foods to eat and which to avoid, as well as recommend supplements, herbs, and lifestyle changes to support gut healing and reduce inflammation. By working together, we can help you restore your health and vitality and improve your overall wellbeing.
