Top Questions

People searching for top rated nutritionists near Bethel Park often ask us these questions.

How long does it take for me to see change?

Everyone is different. We often have to remind people that it took a lifetime to create the challenges that you are facing, so it may take more than a few minutes to restore your health! Our program has worked incredibly well over the years for a large variety of people with various complications and challenges. Each person sees improvements a little differently, but we do focus our program on helping you celebrate the wins very quickly!

Do I have to stop eating what I love?

While there are foods and ingredients that we want to avoid or minimize, the success of Healthy Vibes is not based on the idea of ruining food for you! Our program focuses on making the right changes at the right times to see the right improvements for your personal goals. We will review these together and both commit to the changes we discuss. This helps us keep you accountable as you begin the process of improving your health!

Will I have to be vegan?

While the supplements that we recommend are approved for people with a vegan lifestyle, we do not ask you to become a vegan or vegetarian in order to find success.


Do I have to take medications?

We do not rely on pharmaceuticals as part of our process. Pharmaceuticals unnaturally alter your body's chemistry to produce a desired result. When you stop taking the medications, the results go away. This doesn't really help! We focus on utilizing natural supplements to improve your health while we teach you the good habits that will bring the vitamins from the supplements into your diet.


Are there surgeries or other risky parts to this plan?

We do not ask you to risk your future by undergoing expensive, complicated surgical procedures in order to feel better. While these options may be right for some people, we urge you to work with us first to see if we can help you feel better naturally instead of the alternative of complex surgeries.
